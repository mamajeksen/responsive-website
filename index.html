5 Tipps, to find your way around

At first it seems to be a little bit overwhelming, to find around of all of the sewing and fabric stuff, but in time you get used to everything and it will become your second nature. I have prepared some Questions for you, that will help you to get started:

Which sewing machine do you have?

Depending on which machine do you have, some fabrics can´t be used very well. There are several things to consider, when you buy a sewing machine. Especially when you whant to work with flexible fabrics. Tell me more...

Which characteristics does your fabric needs for your Project?

Should it be breathable, or it is necessary to be waterproof? Do you need a soft feeling oder can it be rough? To answer these questions as good as you can, you have to be shure, want you want to achieve with your project. Before you buy some fabrics, you should research a lot about functionaltiy and usage of them.

What is your Budget?

A lot depends on this question. Good and especially natural fabrics are expensive, with other words they are not affordable for most of the hobby sewers. But there are also some hacks, so you don´t need to buy only cheap or bad qualtiy. Tell me more...

Where should your fabric come from?

If you care about the envoromental Protection, this question will also affect your decision, when you buy some fabrics. Which country made them? Does the Company pays their employee well? Which materials where used? Are the colors colorfast? Sure, all these parameters affects the price and in some points you have to make compromises, but you will drive safer, if you know the answer to all of these questions.

Does the Fabric have a timeless design?

With the Budget in mind, it is smart to think about the longevity of the fabric designs. Depending on which project you are working on, it would be frustrating, if after a few weeks it´s no longer trendy. So, dont let yourself be blinded by fresh and modern colors or patterns, instead buy neutral ones to you save Money. Show me some timeless Fabrics...

Some more Tipps with a long-term view

Think about a Concept

If you are plannig to sew a lot from now on, for example the textiles of your living room, start to think about a concept that runs through all of your projects. That´s how you achieve a harmonious apperance of the final result.

Do researches about the Yarn

In the same Way you have to think about your fabric, you can think about the yarn you use to sew it. There a many parameters to be aware of: The thikness, the material, the color, to just mention some of them. But dont get panic, just take your time to feel good prepared, before you start to sew. It will save you a lot of time, money and nervs.
